# Power-BI-Project-E-commerce

An interactive Power BI Ecommerce Dashboard analyzing online sales performance across Indian states and quarters.
Tracks key KPIs including Total Amount (438K), Total Profit (37K), Sum of Quantity (5615), and Average Order Value (121K).
A donut chart reveals category-wise quantity split — Clothing dominates at 63%, followed by Electronics (21%) and Furniture (17%).
Payment mode analysis shows COD (44%) as the most preferred method, followed by UPI (21%), Debit Card (13%), and Credit Card (12%).
A bar chart compares Sum of Profit and Sum of Amount across states like Maharashtra, Gujarat, Delhi, Kerala, and Madhya Pradesh.
Profit by Month line chart tracks monthly trends from January to December, highlighting seasonal performance patterns.
Sub-category profit analysis shows Printers and Bookcases as top profit-generating sub-categories.
Top customers like Harivansh, Madhav, and Vrinda are visualized by their contribution to total sales amount.
Filters for State (Delhi, Goa, Gujarat, Bihar, etc.) and Quarter (Qtr 1–4) allow deep-dive regional and seasonal analysis.
Built using Power BI and DAX, this dashboard helps e-commerce businesses make fast, data-driven decisions. 🚀
